[b]Neuro Companion[/b]

Neuro Companion is a tModLoader mod that adds a summon companion you can control with Terraria chat commands.

The companion works like a normal summon, but you can also give her simple commands. She can follow you, attack enemies, use a magic weapon, and apply buffs or debuffs.

The mod works without Randy or the Neuro SDK. You can play it as a normal Terraria mod.

If you want to test Neuro/Randy control, the mod can also connect to Randy through websocket. Randy can choose high-level actions, but it does not directly control the player.

[b]Features[/b]

[list]
[*]Summon a Neuro companion minion
[*]Make Neuro follow the player
[*]Attack enemies on command
[*]Start autoattack mode for a limited time
[*]Give Neuro her own magic weapon
[*]Assign Neuro a weapon through commands or the inventory UI
[*]Let Neuro take a valid magic weapon from your inventory
[*]Apply allowed buffs and debuffs
[*]Upgrade the Neuro Companion Staff through Mk2, Mk3, and Mk4
[*]Optionally connect to Randy through websocket for Neuro SDK testing
[/list]

[b]Standalone Use[/b]

Randy is not required.

You can control Neuro with commands such as:

[list]
[*]/neuro help
[*]/neuro status
[*]/neuro follow
[*]/neuro attack
[*]/neuro autoattack 30
[*]/neuro buff ironskin
[*]/neuro debuff enemy poisoned
[*]/neuro weapon status
[*]/neuro weapon inspect
[*]/neuro weapon set
[*]/neuro weapon take
[*]/neuro weapon return
[/list]

[b]Staff Upgrades[/b]

The staff controls how often Neuro can fire her equipped weapon.

[list]
[*]Neuro Companion Staff: 50-tick staff fire limit
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk2: 30-tick staff fire limit
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk3: 1-tick staff fire limit, still limited by the weapon's use time
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk4: 1-tick staff fire limit, still limited by the weapon's use time. Neuro can also detect enemies through blocks, and projectiles fired by Neuro can pass through blocks.
[/list]

Neuro cannot fire faster than the equipped weapon's own use time.

Actual Neuro fire interval = max(staff fire limit, equipped weapon useTime)

[b]Weapon System[/b]

Neuro has her own magic weapon slot.

She can use several kinds of magic weapons:

[list]
[*]Magic weapons that fire normal projectiles
[*]Magic weapons with controllable projectiles, such as Magic Missile
[*]Some simple channeling magic weapons
[*]Magic weapons that place an attack at a target position, such as Resonance Scepter
[/list]

Weapons that create stationary clouds, fields, walls, or similar effects are rejected for now.

Beam weapons such as Last Prism and Laser Machinegun are also rejected for now.

Examples:

[list]
[*]Wand of Sparking - accepted
[*]Water Bolt - accepted
[*]Demon Scythe - accepted
[*]Magic Missile - accepted
[*]Flamelash - accepted
[*]Rainbow Rod - accepted
[*]Resonance Scepter - accepted
[*]Laser Machinegun - rejected
[*]Nimbus Rod - rejected
[*]Clinger Staff - rejected
[*]Magnet Sphere - rejected
[*]Last Prism - rejected
[/list]

[b]Optional Randy/Neuro Integration[/b]

The mod can connect to Randy from the VedalAI Neuro SDK through websocket.

When connected, the mod sends Terraria context and lets Randy/Neuro choose high-level actions. Neuro does not control the player directly.

Randy integration is optional and disabled by default.
