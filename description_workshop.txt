[b]Neuro Companion[/b]

Neuro Companion is a tModLoader mod that adds a command-controlled summon companion to Terraria.

The companion works as a normal summon, but can also be controlled directly through Terraria chat commands. Randy/Neuro integration is optional.

[b]Features[/b]

[list]
[*]Summon a Neuro companion minion
[*]Switch between follow mode and timed autoattack mode
[*]Attack enemies on command
[*]Equip Neuro with her own magic weapon
[*]Use a custom Neuro weapon slot in the inventory UI
[*]Let Neuro take a valid magic weapon from the player inventory
[*]Apply random Red Potion-style buffs to the player
[*]Apply random Red Potion-style debuffs to the player or nearby enemies
[*]Optional Randy websocket integration for Neuro SDK testing
[/list]

[b]Standalone Use[/b]

This mod does not require Neuro, Randy, or the Neuro SDK for normal gameplay.

You can use the companion with commands such as:

[list]
[*]/neuro help
[*]/neuro status
[*]/neuro follow
[*]/neuro attack
[*]/neuro autoattack 30
[*]/neuro weapon status
[*]/neuro weapon inspect
[*]/neuro weapon set
[*]/neuro weapon take
[*]/neuro weapon return
[/list]

[b]Weapon System[/b]

Neuro has her own magic weapon slot.

She currently accepts direct-fire magic weapons, controllable magic weapons, and some channeling magic weapons.

Unsupported support/stationary/persistent-field weapons are rejected.

Examples:

[list]
[*]Wand of Sparking - accepted
[*]Water Bolt - accepted
[*]Demon Scythe - accepted
[*]Magic Missile - accepted
[*]Flamelash - accepted
[*]Rainbow Rod - accepted
[*]Laser Machinegun - rejected
[*]Nimbus Rod - rejected
[*]Clinger Staff - rejected
[*]Magnet Sphere - rejected
[*]Last Prism - rejected for now
[/list]

[b]Optional Randy/Neuro Integration[/b]

The mod can optionally connect to Randy from the VedalAI Neuro SDK through websocket.

When connected, the mod sends Terraria context and lets Randy/Neuro choose high-level actions. Neuro does not directly control the player or press low-level inputs.

Randy integration is optional and disabled by default.

[list]
[*]Neuro Companion Staff: 50-tick staff fire limit
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk2: 30-tick staff fire limit
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk3: as fast as the weapon
[*]Neuro Companion Staff Mk4: as fast as the weapon, Neuro can detect enemies and fire through blocks.
[/list]


[b]Weapon Support[/b]

Neuro currently accepts direct-fire magic weapons, controllable magic weapons, some channeling magic weapons, and targeted-area magic weapons such as Resonance Scepter.

Unsupported support/stationary/persistent-field weapons and held beam weapons are rejected for now.
