Neuro Companion adds a command-controlled summon companion to Terraria.

The companion can follow the player, recall, attack, autoattack, use an equipped magic weapon, and apply buffs or debuffs through chat commands.

Neuro has her own weapon slot and supports direct-fire, controllable, channeling, and targeted-area magic weapons such as Resonance Scepter.

Upgraded staff tiers improve Neuro's firing limits, and Mk4 adds through-block enemy detection plus through-block Neuro projectiles.

The mod works standalone without Neuro/Randy.

Optional Randy websocket integration is included for local Neuro SDK testing, but it is disabled by default.
