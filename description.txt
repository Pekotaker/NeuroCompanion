Neuro Companion adds a summon companion you can control with Terraria chat commands.

The companion can follow the player, attack enemies, autoattack for a limited time, use an equipped magic weapon, and apply buffs or debuffs while summoned.

The mod works as a normal Terraria mod. Neuro/Randy is optional.

Optional Randy websocket integration is included for local Neuro SDK testing, but it is disabled by default.
